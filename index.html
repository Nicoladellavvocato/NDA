Profile

I'm Nicola Dell'Avvocato, an aerospace engineering student at Politecnico di Torino, set on the systems that reach space and hold the edge in defence — launch vehicles, satellites, missiles and uncrewed systems.

My direction is mission analysis and flight dynamics: modelling trajectories and mission performance with Python tools like RocketPy. It sits on real hands-on engineering — I designed and built an F450 quadcopter from airframe to firmware, and ran experimental fibre-optic structural testing on a composite specimen for my thesis. I like problems where simulation meets hardware and the numbers have to hold.

PRJ.01

F450 Quadcopter — Design & Build →

PERSONAL PROJECT · HARDWARE & FIRMWARE

Designed and built an F450-class quadcopter from the airframe up. Structural parts were produced by additive manufacturing and assembled around the F450 frame; I integrated the flight controller, ESCs, power distribution and sensors, and developed the control and telemetry logic in C++. The project spanned mechanical design, embedded electronics and real-time firmware — the full hardware-to-software loop.

PRJ.02

FBG Sensors for Structural Health Monitoring of CFRP →

BSc THESIS · POLITECNICO DI TORINO · 2026

My BSc thesis: experimental structural health monitoring of a carbon-fibre (CFRP) coupon instrumented with Fibre Bragg Grating optical sensors. I prepared and instrumented the specimen, ran load tests and analysed the optical-sensor response to measure strain and detect the onset of damage — hands-on work in composite materials, fibre-optic sensing and experimental validation.

PRJ.03

ISS Live Tracker →

PERSONAL TOOL · ORBITAL TRACKING — SHOWN LIVE ABOVE

A real-time tracker for the International Space Station, built into this site. It reads live orbital data and continuously computes the ISS sub-point — latitude, longitude, altitude and ground speed — plotting its position and trail across the planet. It grew out of my work computing satellite positions in Python with skyfield (TLE and SGP4 propagation).
